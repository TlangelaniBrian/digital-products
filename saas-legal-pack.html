▼

What this does: The mutual liability cap is typical in enterprise SaaS. Note that indemnification is carved out from the cap — this is important because an IP infringement case could involve millions in third-party damages. Enterprise customers often negotiate higher caps (e.g., 2× or 3× annual fees) for enterprise deals. The gross negligence carve-out is essentially required — no court will enforce a cap on wilful bad acts.

Ensure "anonymised" is genuinely anonymised — re-identification risk is a GDPR concern.
