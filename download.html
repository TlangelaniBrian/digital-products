How to receive your download

Thank you for your purchase. Follow the steps below and you'll have your files within 1 hour.

1

Complete your PayPal payment

If you haven't already, go back to the store and click "Buy Now" on your chosen product. You'll be directed to PayPal's secure checkout to complete payment.

2

Get your Transaction ID

After payment, PayPal will send you a confirmation email. Find the Transaction ID — it looks like 5XJ012345AB678901C — and copy it.

3

Send us an email

Email us at mrbtmkhabela@gmail.com with the subject line:
"Order: [Product Name]" — e.g. "Order: SaaS Founder's AI Playbook"

4

Include your Transaction ID in the email body

Paste your PayPal Transaction ID and the name of the product you purchased. This is how we verify your order before releasing your files.

5

Receive your download link

We'll verify your payment and email you a secure download link within 1 hour during business hours (Mon–Fri, UTC). Weekends may take slightly longer.
